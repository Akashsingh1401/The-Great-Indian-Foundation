About us

Established in 2015, we are largest and most trusted philanthropist organisation in the country today. With over 10K+ active members , who try to serve to their full potential towards the welfare of the people of India. This platform allows all the individuals and organisations across the world to help or donate about the cause they care about. Our Community of 1Lakh+ donors and 500+ partners have helped in serving 10Lakh+ people across the country and we aim to keep these numbers increasing, reach out our help to as many as we can.

The famous quote said by Anne Frank - “No one has ever become poor by giving.” Come forward and give little because little changes make huge impact.
